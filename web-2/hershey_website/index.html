Welcome to Hershey Chocolate World

Over more than a century, our iconic brands have been built on a foundation of community investment and connections between people around the world. Equally competitive and compassionate, we continue to build a legacy as we invest in the people and snacks of the future.
Our beloved and indulgent candy brands including Hershey’s, Reese’s, Twizzlers and IceBreakers are joined by a growing family of sweet and salty combinations and better-for-you options like SkinnyPop and Pirates Booty to offer choices in the places and spaces where people shop and snack.

Hersheypark® was originally created by Milton S. Hershey as leisure grounds for employees of Hershey's Chocolate Factory. Today, our family theme park welcomes guests from around the world to experience Hersheypark Happy® all year long!

It's not only the roller coasters and chocolate that make Hershey sweet! For 110 years and counting, Hersheypark has supported Milton Hershey School in its mission of helping students lead happy, healthy, and fulfilling lives - just as our mutual founder, Milton S. Hershey, intended.
