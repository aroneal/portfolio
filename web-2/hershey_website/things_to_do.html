Original Trolley

From Memorial Day to Labor Day, our conductors bring the sights and the inspiring story of Milton Hershey to life in a family-friendly, exciting, and hilarious way on board an old-fashioned Trolley! The whole experience lasts about 45 minutes.

Trick or Treat Trolley

During Halloween in Hershey, journey through Hershey with a show that celebrates the joy of being a kid and Mr. & Mrs. Hershey’s great love for children. While this adventure is not spooky, it does have lots of tricks and treats! The whole experience lasts about 40 minutes.

Holly Jolly Trolley

Take a cozy trip on an old-fashioned trolley which is guaranteed to get you in the Christmas spirit with caroling, stories, and even a special visit from a certain jolly, old elf. The whole experience lasts about 40 minutes.

Sweet Lights Trolley Tour

Looking for an extra special way to celebrate the holidays? Journey through Hershey Sweet Lights, a magical holiday light display, on board an old-fashioned trolley. Our trolley conductor will have carols, Hershey's Kisses Brand Milk Chocolates, and creative commentary to entertain you and your family. It’s about 45-60 minutes in light traffic. On Saturday evenings it can take 90-100 minutes, even with our own dedicated entrance.

Historical Trolley Tour

This in-depth historical tour explores the town built on chocolate and the inspiring story of Milton Hershey. Ride along on an old-fashioned trolley with one of our entertaining and informative conductors. The whole experience lasts about 75 minutes and is the only one that we offer year round.

The Bakery


Visit our bakery for Hershey-inspired homemade desserts and sweet treats made fresh daily. Favorites include exclusive cupcakes, cookies, cakes, brownies and hand-dipped delights (like chocolate-covered popcorn)!

Hershey's Unwrapped


Unwrap and taste your way through the tale of hershey's chocolate with a theatrical experience the whole family will enjoy! From the bean to bar and everything in between, this attraction is sure to dazzle your taste buds, fill you with laughter and sweeten you with hershey heartwarming moments.
